About Kopi Kade

Indulge in the Rich Aromas and Flavors of Exceptional Coffee

At Kopi Kade, we believe that every cup of coffee tells a unique story – from the moment the beans are handpicked to the satisfying sip you take. Our passion for coffee craftsmanship drives us to source the finest beans from around the world and carefully roast them to perfection. The result? A tantalizing experience that awakens your senses and transports you to the lush coffee plantations with every sip.

Discover a World of Coffee Delights

Whether you're a dedicated coffee connoisseur or just starting your coffee journey, Kopi Kade offers an enticing array of options to suit every palate. From our signature blends that harmoniously balance flavors to single-origin selections that highlight the distinct characteristics of various regions, each cup is a voyage of taste and discovery.

More Than Just Coffee

Kopi Kade is more than a coffee shop; it's a community of individuals who share a love for quality coffee, cozy ambiance, and meaningful conversations. Our warm and welcoming space is designed to be your haven – a place to catch up with friends, hold business meetings, or simply escape into a world of relaxation. And to complement your coffee experience, we offer a delectable selection of pastries, sandwiches, and treats, all crafted with the same dedication to quality that defines our coffee.

Our Branches

Kopi Kade has grown strength to strength expanding our branches to Jawatta, Bambalapitiya, Fort, Barnes Place, Nawala, Nugegoda, Weli Park, Pelawaththa, Mount Lavinia, Kandy and Peradeniya with ideas of expanding even further to ensure that a good quality cup of coffee is just within your reach.
